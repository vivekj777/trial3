THEMATIC DANCING




  • Only one team can participate in this event from each group.
  • A maximum number of 15 dancers and a minimum of 8 dancers are allowed in a team.
  • TIME LIMIT: 10 minutes (including stage setup). A warning bell will be rung at 8 minutes indicating two minutes of stage time left. (Minimum time for each group is 4 minutes).
  • 2 marks will be deducted from the total score for every 10 seconds exceeding beyond the above mentioned time.
  • No classical or religious forms of dance are permitted.
  • The theme has to be portrayed in the form of dance only. Teams may perform a free-choice interpretation of a story (theme) and the idea being expressed should be reasonably clear.
  • No use of placards, signs, boards is permitted.
  • No throwing of powdery substance or any other substances including fire presentation is allowed.
  • Animals and vehicles (including bicycles, motorcycles. mopeds) are not allowed.
  • If the group intends to present any certain special effects, the same needs to be informed to the organizers a week prior to the event, failing which the team will be disqualified.
  • Any team found violating any rule will be disqualified
